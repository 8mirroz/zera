## Brand Strategy Definition
- **Target Audience**: Affluent early adopters, founders, architecture-oriented households ($250k+ HHI).
- **Emotional Positioning**: "Spatial Harmony". The robot as a calm gravitational presence, not a mechanical tool.
- **Brand Promise**: Zero-clutter intelligence. A home that anticipates needs without visible hardware friction.
- **Value Proposition**: AURELITH ONE provides "Architectural Motion" – an elegant, sculptural AI hub that manages home routines via soft sensing and kinetic response.
- **Conversion Model**: High-touch "Private Preview" loop. Scarcity and exclusivity-led engagement.
- **Tone of Voice**: Cinematic, restrained, confident, precise.

## Rationale for Motion
The brand's essence is "movement as architecture". This justifies the use of GSAP ScrollTrigger for pinned sequences where the product's internal layers are revealed as the user "architecturally" navigates the page.

## Risks & Decisions
- *Decision*: Rejected "Direct Buy" bouton for a "Book Preview" modal to preserve luxury framing.
- *Risk*: Heavy cinematic hero might increase LCP; mitigated by static poster frame and lazy JS initialization.
